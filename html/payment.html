SIR ROSS MASOOD HALL

Sir Ross Masood Hall was established in the year 1969 in the fond memory of Sir Ross Masood, the grandson of Sir Syed Ahmed Khan. The foundation stone of R.M. Hall was laid by Professor K. A. Nizami. Prof. Abrar Mustafa Khan was the first Provost of this Hall. It comprises of 4 hostel blocks namely Nawab Ismail Khan Hostel (A-Block), SadarYar Jung Hostel (B-Block), Abdul Majeed Khowaja (C-Block) and Habibur Rehman Hostel (D-Block). Now 752 students are registered in this Hall in the session 2020-21.
