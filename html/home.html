Welcome to AMU

A special feature of the University is its residential character with most of the staff and students residing on the campus. AMU is truly representative of the country's multi-religious, multi-racial and multi-lingual character. At Aligarh, Muslims, Hindus, Sikhs and Christians come from all parts of the country - right from Kashmir to Kerala and from Gujarat to Assam - bringing with them different cultural traditions.This traditional and harmonious corporate living has made a major contribution to the development of a broad, cosmopolitan and secular attitude in Aligarh.

Residence Hall For Boys
SIR ROSS MASOD HALL

Sir Ross Masod Hall,established in the year 1969 in the fond memory of Sir Ross Masod, the gradson of Sir Syed Ahmad Khan.

HADI HASAN HALL

Hadi Hasan Hall, for the students of Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College.It is named after Professor Hadi Hasan.

SIR SYED HALL

Sir Syed Hall, the oldest hall of the University.This hall is an example of of harmonious corporate living in the University where each students with his own diverse language and culture live together.

MOHAMMED HABIB HALL

Mohammed Habib Hall,established in the year 1972. this hall originally meant for Research Scholars but with the passage of time, students from various disciplines were admitted.

ALLAM IQBAL BOARDING HOUSE

The foundation stone of Allam Iqbal Boarding House was laid by agreat Islamic Scholar,Malana Syed Abul Hassan Ali Nadvi. this hall is named after Allam Iqbal A great thinker and reowned port.

SIR SHAH SULAIMAN HALL

Sir Shah Sulaiman Hall, is one of the oldest halls of AMU. It accomodates the students of Undergraduate,Post Graduates and as well as Resaerch Scholars of ZHCET.

NADEEM TARIN HALL

Foundation Stone of Nadeem Tarin Hall was laid by the Governor of MP, Mr. Shafi Qureshi on April 7,1993. This hall was named after Mr. Nadeem Tarin, who offered to built a hall of residences at the cost of 2.4 crore.

Residence Hall For Girls
ABDULLAH HALL

Abdullah Hall Comprises of 9 Hostels For girls. it is named after Shiekh Abdullah the pioneer of female education. He was also the founder of Womens College.

BIBI FATMA HALL

The Foundation Stone of Bibi Fatma Hall was laid by Porf. PK Abdul Azis on 27th January,20010 this hall comprises of girl students of B.Tech, B.Arch,M.Tech,B.E.,M.Sc. and PhD.

INDIRA GANDHI HALL

Indira Gandhi Hall, a hall of Residence excusively for female students od Aligarh Muslim University. It was inagurated in 1995. It accomodates the post graduates girl students of University.

SAROJNI NAIDU HALL

This hall was named after the well named the well kniwn freedom fighter and first lady Governor of state in Indepedence India, Mrs. Sarojni Naidu. It was established in the year 1970, with the aim of providing accomodtion to the girls of AMU.
